About

The Artist

Seán O'Meara is an Irish singer-songwriter whose music weaves together intimate storytelling with rich acoustic arrangements. Drawing on the landscapes and spirit of Ireland, his songs carry a warmth and authenticity that has earned him fans across the world.

With his debut album Notions, Potions and Emotions, Seán established himself as one of Ireland's most compelling emerging voices in folk and acoustic music.
